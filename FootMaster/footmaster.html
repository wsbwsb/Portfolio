FOOTMASTER has been manufacturing casters & wheels. We have a reputation for excellence by supplying domestic and foreign customers with not only functional quality casters but also general casters such as light, medium and heavy duty casters. FOOTMASTER provide for ease of movement and level setting, for a wide variety of machines, equipment, and work stations. We are specializing leveling caster, shock absorbing caster, precision caster and caster wheel.
